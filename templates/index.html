Built for everyday image work

Format-aware compression

JPEG uses quality-based re-encoding, PNG uses palette reduction and compression level, WEBP uses its native quality codec — each format is handled the way it's meant to be.

Your own history

Every upload is saved to your account with original size, compressed size, and percent saved, so you can track it over time.

Optional resizing

Cap large photos at 1920px on their longest side for extra savings when you don't need full resolution.
